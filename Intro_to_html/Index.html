Grey's Anatomy

Grey's Anatomy is a long-running medical drama series that first premiered in 2005. Created by Shonda Rhimes, the show is set at the fictional Seattle Grace, later known as Grey Sloan Mermorial, Hospital. The show follows the personal and professional lives of surgical interns, residents, and Surgeons.
